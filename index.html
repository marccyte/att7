About Me

Message for the Readers


If you’ve ever found yourself thinking a subject is “too hard,” allow me to offer a different perspective: every complex concept can be understood — it just needs to be broken down the right way.

I’m Marc Lorens R. Fernandez, a pre-service teacher who believes that learning should be clear, empowering, and accessible to all. This e-portfolio is a reflection of my practice teaching journey at General Emilio Aguinaldo National High School (GEANHS) in Imus, Cavite, during the fourth quarter of the school year 2024–2025. It captures my teaching philosophy, instructional strategies, and the meaningful moments that shaped my growth as an educator.

Inside, you’ll find curated lesson plans, classroom experiences, reflections, and student work — all of which aim to demonstrate how I strive to make learning not just effective, but engaging and encouraging.

Whether you are a fellow educator, mentor, or learner, I hope this portfolio offers insight into the kind of teacher I aspire to be. So, ready to see how "basic" learning can be? Let's go!
