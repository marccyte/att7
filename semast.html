SEMAST

Purpose:

This self-assessment instrument is designed for use of those who are being trained to join the teaching profession and those who are already teaching. The instrument could be used as a measure of their belief of their capability in attaining the standards set for beginning and proficient teachers as defined in the Philippine Professional Standards for Teachers (PPST). It is important for a teacher to meet the standards of the teaching profession as this is a possible basis in designing their professional development programs and in gauging the effectiveness of their instruction.
